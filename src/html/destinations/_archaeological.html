Archaeological Sites

Journey back in time to Mexico's ancient civilizations as you explore awe-inspiring archaeological sites, where towering pyramids, intricate temples, and sacred ruins tell the stories of past empires and lost civilizations.

Types of Destinations

  • Chichen Itza

    Chichen Itza, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the New Seven Wonders of the World, mesmerizes visitors with its iconic pyramid, El Castillo, and intricate stone carvings that reveal the ancient Maya's astronomical and architectural achievements. Explore the Temple of the Warriors, the Great Ball Court, and the Sacred Cenote, uncovering the mysteries of this ancient city and its cultural significance.

    Learn More

  • Teotihuacan

    Teotihuacan, the "City of the Gods," boasts majestic pyramids, including the towering Pyramid of the Sun and the Pyramid of the Moon, set against the backdrop of the Mexican highlands. Ascend to the top of these ancient structures for panoramic views of the surrounding landscape, and explore the Avenue of the Dead, the Temple of the Feathered Serpent, and other archaeological treasures that offer insights into the cosmology and daily life of the Teotihuacan civilization.
