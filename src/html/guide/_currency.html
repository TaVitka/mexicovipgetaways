Mexico's official currency is the Mexican Peso (MXN), denoted by the symbol "$".

Currency exchange is widely available at banks, exchange offices, and hotels throughout the country, with the US Dollar being the most commonly accepted foreign currency.

  • Exchange Money at Banks

    Utilize banks for currency exchange as they often offer more favorable rates compared to exchange offices and hotels.

  • Use ATMs Wisely

    Opt for ATMs located within banks or reputable establishments to avoid potential scams or fraudulent activities.

  • Manage Finances Securely

    Keep track of your expenses and monitor your bank statements regularly to ensure your financial transactions are secure and accurate throughout your travels in Mexico.
